Mayday is a Taiwanese pop rock band founded on March 29, 1997. It consists of five members: Wen Shangyi (Monster), Chen Xinhong (Ashin), Shi Jinhang (Stone), Cai Shengyan (Masha), and Liu Yanming (Guanyou). In 1998, they signed with Rolling Stone Records. In 1999, they released their first music album, "The First Original Album".
